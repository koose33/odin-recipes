Cilantro Lime Tacos

Fresh and zesty street-style tacos that bring authentic Mexican flavors to your kitchen. Tender seasoned meat is topped with a bright cilantro-lime sauce, crisp cabbage slaw, and creamy avocado. These tacos are perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or casual gathering with friends. The secret is in the marinade - lime juice and cilantro work together to tenderize the meat while infusing it with bold citrus flavor. Serve with warm corn tortillas and your favorite toppings for a customizable meal everyone will love.

INGREDIENTS

  • Tortillas
  • Grilled Meat
  • Cilantro
  • Limes
  • Tomatoes

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Cook meat in skillet
  2. Warm the tortillas on pan or in microwave
  3. Fill each tortilla with meat and topping
  4. Add salsa, lime, or sour cream and serve
