Breakfast Burger

This isn't your average burger. It's breakfast and lunch combined into one indulgent masterpiece. A juicy beef patty is topped with crispy bacon, a perfectly fried egg, melted cheese, and hash browns all stacked between a toasted bun. It's the ultimate comfort food for those mornings when cereal just won't cut it. The runny egg yolk acts as a rich sauce that brings all the components together. Best enjoyed with a side of crispy fries and plenty of napkins, because this burger is delightfully messy.

INGREDIENTS

  • Ground Beef
  • Burger Buns
  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Cheddar Cheese

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Form beef into patties
  2. Cook bacon and add patties cooking 4-5 minutes per side
  3. Add cheese to burgers before removing
  4. Fry eggs and put over pattie when assembling burger
