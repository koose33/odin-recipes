BBQ Chicken Pizza

A sweet and savory twist on traditional pizza that has become a crowd favorite. Tangy barbecue sauce replaces marinara as the base, topped with tender chicken, red onions, fresh cilantro, and a blend of mozzarella and gouda cheeses. The combination creates a perfect balance of smoky, sweet, and cheesy flavors in every bite. Originally popularized by California Pizza Kitchen, this pizza proves that thinking outside the traditional Italian box can lead to delicious results. It's an excellent choice for pizza night when you want something a little different from the usual pepperoni.

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken
  • Pizza Dough
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Gouda Cheese
  • Red Onion
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Olive Oil

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F
  2. Roll out pizza dough and brush lightly with olive oil and BBQ sauce
  3. Top with shredded cooked chicken and red onion slices then sprinkle with cheese
  4. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes and serve
